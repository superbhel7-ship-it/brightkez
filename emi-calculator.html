Loan Information

Understanding Home Loans

Interest Rates

Current home loan interest rates range from 8% to 10% depending on your credit score and bank.

Loan Tenure

Most banks offer home loans with tenures ranging from 5 to 30 years. Longer tenure means lower EMI.

Processing Fees

Banks typically charge 0.5% to 1% of the loan amount as processing fees.

Down Payment

Usually, banks finance 80-90% of property value. You need to arrange 10-20% as down payment.

Expert Tips

Tips for Home Loan Approval

1

Maintain Good Credit Score

A credit score above 750 increases your chances of loan approval and better interest rates.

2

Keep Documents Ready

Have your income proof, bank statements, and property documents organized for faster processing.

3

Compare Multiple Lenders

Shop around for the best interest rates and terms from different banks and NBFCs.

4

Choose Optimal EMI

Your EMI should not exceed 40% of your monthly income for comfortable repayment.
